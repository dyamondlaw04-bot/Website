Industry of Interest

The industry that interests me is the cybersecurity field within Information Technology. Cybersecurity focuses on protecting computers, networks, systems, and private information from hackers, malware, scams, and other online threats. This industry is important because almost every business, school, hospital, bank, and government agency depends on technology to store and protect information.

Career That Interests Me

The career I am interested in is becoming a Cybersecurity Analyst. A Cybersecurity Analyst helps protect an organization by monitoring computer systems, checking for suspicious activity, investigating security alerts, and helping prevent cyberattacks. This career interests me because it allows me to solve problems, protect people’s information, and work in a field that continues to grow as technology becomes more advanced.

How Cybersecurity and AI Impact My Career Choice

Cybersecurity impacts my career choice because it is the main purpose of the job. As a future Cybersecurity Analyst, I would need to understand how cybercriminals attack systems and how to defend against those attacks. Artificial Intelligence also impacts this career because AI can help detect threats faster, recognize unusual behavior, and analyze large amounts of data. However, AI can also be used by attackers to create stronger scams, fake messages, and automated attacks. This makes me even more interested in cybersecurity because the field requires constant learning, responsibility, and ethical decision-making.
