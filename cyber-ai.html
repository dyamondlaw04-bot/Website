Privacy Concerns

One ethical issue related to AI and cybersecurity is protecting user privacy. AI systems often collect and analyze large amounts of personal information. If data is mishandled, individuals can lose privacy and trust.

My values of honesty and respect guide me to handle information responsibly and protect personal data whenever possible.

Bias in Artificial Intelligence

Another ethical issue is bias in AI systems. If an AI system is trained using biased data, it can produce unfair results that impact hiring, lending, healthcare, and other important decisions.

My values of integrity and responsibility encourage me to identify potential bias and work toward fair and ethical solutions.
